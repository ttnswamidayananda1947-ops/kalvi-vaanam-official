ЁЯУШ Kalvi Vaanam тАУ History (English)

Kalvi Vaanam is an educational website created for students studying at TT Narasimman Swami Dayananda School. The main goal of this website is to support students by providing useful educational resources in one place.

This platform was developed by a student community with the guidance and vision of teachers. The idea was to help students access question papers, study materials, and academic guidance easily after examinations.

The technical development of Kalvi Vaanam was done with the help of ChatGPT (Artificial Intelligence). By learning and applying web technologies such as HTML and CSS, the entire website was successfully completed within two months.

Kalvi Vaanam is not just a website тАФ it is a student-driven educational initiative created with the intention of promoting learning and knowledge sharing.
